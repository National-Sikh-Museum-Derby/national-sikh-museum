ਰਾਸ਼ਟਰੀ ਸਿੱਖ ਅਜਾਇਬਘਰ
NATIONAL SIKH MUSEUM DERBY UK

ਖੁੱਲੇ ਦਰਸ਼ਨ ਦੀਦਾਰੇ, ਸਿੰਘਾਂ ਦੇ ਤੋਪ ਖਾਂਨਿਆਂ ਦੇ। ਵਿਰਾਸਤ -ਏ- ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ। ਇਤਿਹਾਸਕ ਨਜ਼ਰ, ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ ਰਾਜ ਬਣਾਉਣ ਵਾਲੇ ਸਰਦਾਰ ਤੇ ਢਾਉਣ ਵਾਲੇ ਗਦਾਰ। A modern, packed, multi-channel museum with real artefacts that allow the visitor to appreciate the rich and complex heritage of the Sikhs in a story of courage, sacrifice and bloody genocide.

Open 7 days a week - 10AM to 3PM

Group bookings by appointment only

ਜ਼ਫ਼ਰਨਾਮਾ - The Zafarnāma - ظفرنامہ, (Declaration of Victory) letter sent by Guru Gobind Singh Ji in 1705 to the Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb.

What you'll discover

ਤੋਪ-ਖ਼ਾਨੇ
Heavy Artillery

Visitors can expect to see historic cannons on display at the museum, accompanied by information about their significance and history.

ਸਰਕਾਰ -ਏ- ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ
Art Gallary

The museum's art gallery showcases paintings, sculptures, and other works of art related to Sikh history and culture.

ਇਤਿਹਾਸਕ ਖਜ਼ਾਨੇ
Punjab Heritage

See various old-style items that provide a glimpse into Sikh culture and way of life, such as traditional clothing, household items, and more.

ਵਿਰਾਸਤ -ਏ- ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ
Sikh Empire Artefacts

View artefacts from the Sikh Empire, including weapons, coins, documents, and other historical items that offer insight into the empire's history and culture.

ਨਸਲਕੁਸ਼ੀ
Genocide Exhibtions

Exhibtion related to the 1984 Sikh Genocide, which could include photographs, documents, and personal accounts of the tragic events.

ਜਲ੍ਹਿਆਂ ਵਾਲ਼ਾ ਬਾਗ
Jallianwala Bagh

Learn about the 1919 Jallianwala Bagh Massacre, which can include historical accounts, photographs, and artifacts related to the event.

ਜੰਗੀ ਹਥਿਆਰ
Old Weapons

Visitors can view old weapons used by Sikhs in battles, including swords, shields, and other traditional weapons.

ਦਰਬਾਰ ਸਾਹਿਬ ਮਾਡਲ
Golden Temple Model

One of the most important religious sites for Sikhs. A model which provides a unique and detailed view of the temple's architecture.

ਐਂਗਲੋ-ਸਿੱਖ ਸਬੰਧ
Anglo-Sikh Relations

Exhibition and information on the role Sikhs played in the British Empire, including their contributions to the military and economy.

ਇਤਿਹਾਸਿਕ ਦਸਤਾਵੇਜ਼
Reference Library

Visitors can access a library for books related to Sikh history and culture, which provide a wealth of information and insights into the Sikhs.
