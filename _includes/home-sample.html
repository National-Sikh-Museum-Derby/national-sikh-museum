ਰਾਸ਼ਟਰੀ ਸਿੱਖ ਅਜਾਇਬਘਰ - ਡਰਬੀ ਯੂਕੇ
NATIONAL SIKH MUSEUM - DERBY UK

ਖੁੱਲੇ ਦਰਸ਼ਨ ਦੀਦਾਰੇ, ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ ਤੋਪ ਖਾਂਨਿਆਂ ਦੇ। ਵਿਰਾਸਤ -ਏ- ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ। ਇਤਿਹਾਸਕ ਨਜ਼ਰ, ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ ਰਾਜ ਕਿਵੇਂ ਆਇਆ, ਕਿਵੇਂ ਤੇ ਕਿਉਂ ਗਿਆ। ਧੌਂਸਾ ਵੱਜਿਆ ਕੂਚ ਦਾ ਹੁਕਮ ਹੋਇਆ, ਚੜ੍ਹੇ ਸੂਰਮੇ ਸਿੰਘ ਦਲੇਰ ਮੀਆਂ । ਚੜ੍ਹੇ ਪੁੱਤਰ ਸਰਦਾਰਾਂ ਦੇ ਛੈਲ ਬਾਂਕੇ, ਜੈਸੇ ਬੇਲਿਓਂ ਨਿਕਲਦੇ ਸ਼ੇਰ ਮੀਆਂ । ਚੜ੍ਹੇ ਸਭ ਮਝੈਲ ਦੁਆਬੀਏ ਜੀ, ਜਿਨ੍ਹਾਂ ਕਿਲ੍ਹੇ ਨਿਵਾਏ ਨੇ ਢੇਰ ਮੀਆਂ । ‘ਸ਼ਾਹ ਮੁਹੰਮਦਾ’ ਤੁਰੇ ਜੰਬੂਰ ਖਾਨੇ, ਹੋਇਆ ਹੁਕਮ ਨਾ ਲਾਂਵਦੇ ਦੇਰ ਮੀਆਂ । A modern, packed, multi-channel museum with real artefacts that allow the visitor to appreciate the rich and complex heritage of the Sikhs in a story of courage, sacrifice and bloody genocide.

Open 7 days a week (Free Admission) - 10AM to 3PM

Group bookings by appointment only

ਪੰਛੀ ਝਾਤ - What you'll discover

ਤੋਪ-ਖ਼ਾਨੇ
Heavy Artillery

Visitors can expect to see historic cannons on display at the museum, accompanied by information about their significance and history.

ਸਰਕਾਰ -ਏ- ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ
Art Gallery

The museum's art gallery showcases paintings, sculptures, and other works of art related to Sikh history and culture.

ਇਤਿਹਾਸਕ ਖ਼ਜਾਨੇ
Punjab Heritage

See various items of heritage that provide a glimpse into Sikh culture and way of life, such as traditional clothing, household, and more.

ਵਿਰਾਸਤ -ਏ- ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ
Sikh Empire Artefacts

View artefacts from the Sikh Empire, includings coins, documents, and other historical items that offer insight into the empire's history and culture.

ਸਿੱਖ ਨਸਲਕੁਸ਼ੀ
Genocide Exhibitions

Exhibiting history of Sikh Genocide including 1984, photographs, documents and personal accounts of the tragic events.

ਜਲ੍ਹਿਆਂ ਵਾਲ਼ਾ ਬਾਗ਼
Jallianwala Bagh

Learn about the 1919 Jallianwala Bagh Massacre, which can include historical accounts, photographs, and artefacts related to the event.

ਜੰਗੀ ਹਥਿਆਰ
Light Arms

Visitors can view light arms used by Sikhs in battles, including swords, shields, and other traditional weapons.

ਸ਼੍ਰੀ ਦਰਬਾਰ ਸਾਹਿਬ ਅਤੇ ਸ਼੍ਰੀ ਅਕਾਲ ਤਖ਼ਤ ਸਾਹਿਬ ਦਾ ਮਾਡਲ
Golden Temple & Akal Takht Model

One of the most important religious sites for Sikhs.

ਐਂਗਲੋ-ਸਿੱਖ ਸੰਬੰਧ
Anglo-Sikh Relations

Exhibition and information on the role Sikhs played in the British Empire, including their contributions to the military and economy.

ਇਤਿਹਾਸਿਕ ਦਸਤਾਵੇਜ਼
Reference Library

Visitors can access a library for books related to Sikh history and culture, which provide a wealth of information and insights into the Sikhs.

ਦਰਬਾਰ-ਏ-ਖ਼ਾਲਸਾ
Theatre

Here visitors shall be inspired by social/religious aspects of the very rich heritage delivered in the form of lectures/speeches along with audio/video productions.

ਸ਼ਹੀਦੀ ਯਾਦਗਾਰ - ਫ਼ਤਿਹ ਦਰਵਾਜਾ
Memorial

A very unique and meaningful memorial dedicated to all Sikh campaigns and to all those who lost their lives defending the principles of Sikhi.
